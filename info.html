hi@trekmatthews.com

Trek Matthews is an illustrator, painter, and designer based in Chicago. His personal work focuses on arranging spaces and forms as travelogue, by using letterforms and architectural reference collected between destinations.

Trek has worked with commercial clients such as Dolby Laboratories, Mailchimp, Waldorf Astoria, and W Hotels. He has also had the pleasure to work on more intimate projects with Daedelus, Bleep Labs, Odd Pears, and Eskmo, in addition to assisting design studios.
